Who are We?

Our Roots:

Founded in 2010 by health enthusiasts and fruit aficionados, Bountiful Foods emerged from a shared passion for creating delicious and nutritious beverages. Nestled in the sunny landscapes of California, our founders envisioned a place where the abundance of local, farm-fresh produce could be transformed into delightful smoothie concoctions that would not only tantalize taste buds but also contribute to a healthy lifestyle.

Crafting the Perfect Blend:

From the very beginning, our commitment has been to craft the perfect blend of flavors, using only the finest, locally sourced ingredients. With a dedication to quality and a love for all things fresh, Bountiful Foods quickly became a go-to destination for those seeking a tasty escape into the world of wholesome smoothies.

California Vibes, Global Tastes:

Inspired by the diverse and vibrant culture of California, our menu reflects a fusion of global tastes with a local twist. Each smoothie is a celebration of the Golden State’s rich agricultural tapestry, bringing together flavors from the orchards, fields, and vineyards that define the California experience.

A Sustainable Commitment:

As stewards of the environment, we take pride in our sustainable practices. Bountiful Foods is committed to reducing our ecological footprint by sourcing responsibly, minimizing waste, and promoting eco-friendly initiatives. It's not just about nourishing our customers; it's about nourishing the planet we all call home.

Community-Centric Values:

Beyond the blender, Bountiful Foods is deeply rooted in community. We cherish the relationships we’ve built with local farmers, customers, and neighborhoods throughout the years. Our commitment to giving back extends beyond the cup, as we actively participate in community events, support local charities, and contribute to the well-being of the people who make California a truly special place.

The Future of Freshness:

As we continue to blend innovation with tradition, Bountiful Foods looks toward the future with excitement and anticipation. Our commitment to freshness, flavor, and community remains unwavering, and we invite you to join us on this delicious journey.


Thank you for being a part of the Bountiful Foods story. Here’s to many more years of blending joy, health, and the California spirit in every sip!
