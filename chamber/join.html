Which Tier is Right For Me?

Non-Profit Tier

  • Networking Opportunities: Access to basic networking events and online forums.
  • Information Resources: Monthly newsletter and access to basic online resources.
  • Event Discounts: Discounts on select Chamber events.
  • Promotional Opportunities: Listing in the Chamber's online directory.

Bronze Tier

  • All Non-Profit Tier Benefits
  • Enhanced Networking Opportunities: Access to additional, exclusive networking events.
  • Advertising Discounts: Discounts on advertising in Chamber publications.
  • Event Hosting: Opportunity to host a Chamber event once a year.
  • Business Support: Basic business counseling and support services.

Silver Tier

  • All Bronze Tier Benefits
  • Increased Visibility: Featured business profile on Chamber's website and newsletters.
  • Advanced Business Support: Access to advanced business workshops and webinars.
  • Sponsorship Opportunities: Eligibility to sponsor Chamber events.
  • Community Leadership Opportunities: Participation in Chamber committees.

Gold Tier

  • All Silver Tier Benefits
  • Premier Networking Events: Invitations to high-profile networking events and roundtables.
  • One-on-One Business Coaching: Personalized business coaching and mentoring.
  • Leadership Recognition: Recognition as a leading member in Chamber publications and events.
  • Customized Support: Tailored support services to meet specific business needs.
